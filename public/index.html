ABOUT ME

I'm a professional with a passion for web development, technology, and artistic development. I've had a years worth of experience in HTML/CSS, prior to learning Full Stack Web Development. As a result, I now make fully-functional web applications for businesses in need of my services. I believe in building key relationships with my clients that last a lifetime!

Jaylon Ballard

Frontend Web Developer

A native of Miami, Florida, Jaylon is a frontend developer and designer who likes to use his creativity to bring out the best in any project he takes on. Being from such an artistically saturated city, its no surprise that Jaylon uses a lot of cultural and sub-cultural influence within his artistic style.

At age 10, he took an interest in the graffiti scene around his Coconut Grove neighborhood, which served as his introduction into a world of infinite artistic possibilities. Jaylon was later introduced to web development by a great friend, mentor, and colleague of his. It was only a matter of time before he gained an interest in frontend development.

Now in his early 20s, Jaylon works adamantly to create and curate art in a manner that he feels will not only express himself, but also in a way that will help bring alive the vision of countless others. As a frontend specialist, he hopes to serve a vast array of clientele and help bring their dreams to fruition.
